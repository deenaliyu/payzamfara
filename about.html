What you need to know About IBS

The Intelligent Billing System (IBS)

Is an intelligent, powerful, and robust central platform for tax management. It enables billing/tax monitoring, e-invoicing, and e-payment for the State Board of Internal Revenue linked with all MDAs for proper alignment, codification, and classification of receivables and collections into the State revenue heads and Treasury Single Account(s) -TSA.

What are our strategic objectives?

IBS is integrating technology to the processes of the State Board of Internal Revenue. We wish to achieve the
following objectives.

  • Establish systems for administering taxes and income that are effective, fair, and accommodating to individuals at all levels, in all MDAs, and other State entities that generate money.
  • Ensure that all chargeable persons are assessed to pay taxes in line with the law and based on objective criteria to achieve justice and equality.
  • Eliminate inefficiencies in the assessment, collection, and reporting procedures, and make sure that all funds are directed to the appropriate government accounts.
  • Ensure that taxpayer assessments are accurate and comprehensive.
  • Utilize technology to ensure that government agencies may view invoices and payments made by their organizations in real time.
  • Assure users of the quick revenue platform with 24-hour support.
