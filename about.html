What you need to know About IBS

The Integrated Billing System (IBS)

Revolutionizes tax management by serving as a cutting-edge, intelligent, and robust central platform. It encompasses a range of features including billing/tax monitoring, e-invoicing, and e-payment, specifically designed for the State Board of Internal Revenue. By seamlessly integrating with all MDAs, it facilitates proper alignment, codification, and classification of receivables and collections, ultimately channeling them into the State revenue heads and Treasury Single Account(s) -TSA.

What are our strategic objectives?

IBS is integrating technology to the processes of the State Board of Internal Revenue. We wish to achieve the
following objectives.

  • Modernize tax administration: IBS leverages advanced technology to transform the processes of the State Board of Internal Revenue. Our primary goal is to establish effective, fair, and inclusive systems for administering taxes and income across all levels, MDAs, and other State entities responsible for generating revenue.
  • Equitable assessment and taxation: We strive to ensure that all chargeable individuals are assessed to pay taxes in accordance with the law and based on objective criteria. Our objective is to promote justice and equality by implementing a transparent and unbiased approach to taxation.
  • Streamline procedures and maximize revenue: IBS eliminates inefficiencies in the assessment, collection, and reporting procedures, ensuring that all funds are accurately directed to the appropriate government accounts. By optimizing the workflow, we aim to enhance revenue collection and utilization.
  • Accurate and comprehensive taxpayer assessments: Through the utilization of technology, IBS guarantees precise and comprehensive assessments of taxpayers. This ensures that tax calculations are accurate, reducing errors and discrepancies.
  • Real-time visibility for government agencies: IBS empowers government agencies by providing them with real-time access to view invoices and payments made by their respective organizations. This transparency allows for efficient monitoring and enables prompt decision-making.
  • Reliable revenue platform with 24-hour support: We prioritize user satisfaction and offer a dependable revenue platform supported by round-the-clock assistance. This ensures that users can access and utilize IBS at any time, addressing their concerns and providing timely support.

By accomplishing these strategic objectives, IBS revolutionizes tax management for the State Board of Internal Revenue, fostering transparency, efficiency, and accountability throughout the process.
